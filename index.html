What is VOTUM?

VOTUM is a sovereign, transparent and verifiable digital voting protocol. It aims to support democratic systems with secure and auditable blockchain-based tools. Designed for governments, organizations, and local authorities, VOTUM empowers civic governance in the digital age.

Why this Token?

To ensure full independence, we are creating a legal and technical infrastructure. The $VOTUM token represents a symbolic and financial support for this initiative. It allows us to remain neutral and resilient.

What are you supporting?

  • The creation of an independent organization based in France
  • The development of a public open-source protocol
  • Audit trails and transparency for voting results
  • The emergence of decentralized, civic-based governance

Disclaimer

The $VOTUM token does not represent an official voting right. It is a symbolic support token and does not promise yield, power, or authority. It is not an investment product.
