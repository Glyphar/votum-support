Why this token?

VOTUM is a sovereign, transparent and verifiable digital voting protocol. To ensure full independence, we need the means to create a proper legal framework, conduct audits, and launch pilot programs. The $VOTUM token is a citizen-powered initiative meant to support this construction.

What your support empowers

  • The creation of an independent legal structure for VOTUM
  • The open-source development of the voting protocol
  • The transparency and traceability of decision-making systems
  • The rise of global, citizen-led governance models

Important Notice

The $VOTUM token is not an official voting right. It does not replace the $VOTE token used in the protocol. It stands as a symbolic and economic gesture of civic support. There is no promise of return or power.
