Why this token?

VOTUM is a sovereign, transparent and verifiable digital voting protocol. To guarantee its complete independence, we must gather the necessary means to establish the legal structure, conduct audits, and launch pilot programs. The $VOTUM token is a citizen-led initiative to support the foundation of this public infrastructure.

What your support enables

  • Creating a legal structure compliant with French and European regulations
  • Developing the open-source protocol
  • Ensuring transparency and traceability of voting and decision systems
  • Encouraging decentralized governance models run by citizens

Important Note

The $VOTUM token is not an official voting right. It does not replace the $VOTE token used in the protocol. It represents a symbolic and economic gesture of civic support. It carries no promise of financial return or decision-making power.
